Lab 9: Libraries & jQuery

challenge

The primary challenge in this week's assignment was incorporating jQuery into a functional webpage. My partner and I decided to experiment with dynamically manipulating DOM elements using jQuery, which was a new and somewhat complex concept for us.

problems

At first, our script did not execute as expected. This was primarily due to the placement of our jQuery script tag. We learned the hard way that jQuery must be loaded before our custom scripts.

reflection

I learned the importance of understanding the sequence in which scripts load and the nuances of jQuery syntax and selectors. It was enlightening to see how a few lines of jQuery could significantly alter the behavior and style of a webpage without altering the underlying HTML structure.
